## Overview
Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) examining the relationship between economic stress indicators and mental health outcomes across 19 Latin American countries. Variables: GDP per capita, unemployment rate, inflation, Gini coefficient, poverty rate, economic stress index, depression prevalence, anxiety prevalence, and psychological well-being index. Sources: IMF, World Bank, WHO, PAHO.
