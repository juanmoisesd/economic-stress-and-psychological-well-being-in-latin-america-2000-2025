### New Open Dataset Published: Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) examining the relationship between economic stress indicators and mental health outcomes across 19 Latin American countries. Variables: GDP per capita, unemployment rate, inflation, Gini coefficient, poverty rate, eco

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Longitudinal dataset (2000-2025) examining the relationship between economic stress indicators and mental health outcomes across 19 Latin American countries. Variables: GDP per capita, unemployment rate, inflation, Gini coefficient, poverty rate, eco*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
